Welcome to Our Digital Precinct

The River City Police Department is dedicated to ensuring a safe and secure environment for all residents, businesses, and visitors. We strive to build strong relationships with the community through transparency, fairness, and dedicated service.

This website is your resource for non-emergency services, crime prevention tips, department news, and information on joining our team. Together, we can make River City a better and safer place.

Core Department Resources

Patrol Division

Our uniformed officers are the backbone of the RCPD, providing rapid response and maintaining a highly visible presence throughout all city districts 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Patrol officers focus on crime prevention, emergency response, traffic enforcement, and community engagement. They are dedicated to ensuring the immediate safety and security of River City.

Need to Report a Non-Emergency?

For non-life threatening incidents, property crimes, or general inquiries (like noise complaints or illegal parking), please use our dedicated non-emergency line.

CALL: 3-1-1

Youth & Community Programs

We believe in building bridges with the next generation. Our programs focus on mentorship, safety education, and positive interactions with local youth.

Check out our Police Athletic League (PAL) and Safety Cadets for opportunities for young people to engage positively with the department and learn valuable life skills.

Crime Prevention & Safety Tips

Protecting yourself and your property starts with awareness. We provide up-to-date resources and guides to help River City residents stay safe.

Topics include neighborhood watch setup, securing your home from break-ins, and online safety for families and students.

Transparency & Accountability

The RCPD is committed to open communication with the public. We strive for accountability in all our actions and decision-making.

Access departmental reports, policies, and community feedback forms through our Open Records portal.
